---
title: "Manage Subscription"
description: "View your subscription status, cancel, renew, or update payment details from the Billing page."
---

All subscription management is available from **Billing** in the sidebar.

## Viewing your subscription status

The Billing page shows:

- Whether you are currently subscribed
- Your next billing date
- Your subscription status (for example, `active` or `on_hold`)
- Whether auto-renewal is enabled

## Cancelling your subscription

Click **Cancel subscription** on the Billing page. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period — you are not cut off immediately.

<Warning>
  Cancelling stops auto-renewal but does not immediately remove your access. You keep all Pro features until your current period ends.
</Warning>

## Renewing your subscription

If you previously cancelled and want to re-enable auto-renewal before your billing period ends, click **Renew subscription** on the Billing page.

## Managing payment methods and invoices

Click the **Customer Portal** link on the Billing page to:

- Update your payment method
- View past invoices
- Review billing history

## Subscription on hold

If your subscription status shows `on_hold`, this typically indicates a payment issue. Open the **Customer Portal** from the Billing page and update your payment method to restore access.
