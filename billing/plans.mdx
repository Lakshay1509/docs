Neatmail offers a free tier alongside a Pro subscription that unlocks the full feature set.

## Free tier

You can use Neatmail without a subscription. However, the following features require an active subscription:

- Auto-labeling
- AI draft generation
- Custom labels
- Label settings and draft preferences

## Pro subscription

A Pro subscription gives you full access to every feature in Neatmail:

- **Auto-labeling** — incoming emails are categorized and labeled in real time
- **AI drafts** — Neatmail generates reply drafts in your tone using GPT-4 Mini
- **Custom labels** — create labels with your own name, color, and description
- **All settings** — full control over label categories, draft preferences, and integrations

## How to subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Go to **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start checkout">
    Click **Join now**. You will be redirected to the checkout page, where billing is handled securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your plan">
    After subscribing, your plan status appears on the Billing page, along with your next billing date and renewal status.
  </Step>
</Steps>
