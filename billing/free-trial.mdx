Neatmail gives you a 7-day free trial so you can explore every Pro feature before committing to a subscription.

## What the trial includes

The free trial provides full access to all Neatmail features:

- Auto-labeling
- AI draft generation
- Custom labels
- All settings

No payment is required to start your trial.

## Starting your free trial

On the Billing page, click **Start Free Trial**. Your trial activates immediately and full Pro access begins.

## Tracking your trial

While your trial is active, the Billing page shows a progress bar indicating how many days remain. Your trial end date is displayed below the progress bar.

## When the trial ends

When the 7-day trial period ends, premium features are paused until you subscribe. Your email account remains connected and your settings are preserved.

<Warning>
  If you don't subscribe before the trial ends, auto-labeling, AI drafts, and custom labels will stop working.
</Warning>

## Converting your trial to a paid subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Go to **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe">
    Click **Join now**. You will be redirected to the checkout page to complete your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>
