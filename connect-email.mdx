---
title: "Connect Email"
description: "Link your Gmail or Outlook account to Neatmail using OAuth."
---

Neatmail supports Gmail and Outlook. You connect your account once using OAuth — you'll be redirected to Google or Microsoft to authorize access, and Neatmail never sees or stores your password.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Gmail">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to neatmail.app">
        Sign in to your Neatmail account at [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Connect Gmail">
        On the connection screen, select **Connect Gmail**. You'll be redirected to Google's authorization page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authorize access">
        Sign in with your Google account if prompted, then review the permissions Neatmail is requesting and click **Allow**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Return to Neatmail">
        After authorizing, you'll be returned to Neatmail automatically. The onboarding modal will open so you can choose your email categories.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Outlook">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to neatmail.app">
        Sign in to your Neatmail account at [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Connect Outlook">
        On the connection screen, select **Connect Outlook**. You'll be redirected to Microsoft's authorization page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authorize access">
        Sign in with your Microsoft account if prompted, then review the permissions Neatmail is requesting and click **Accept**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Return to Neatmail">
        After authorizing, you'll be returned to Neatmail automatically. The onboarding modal will open so you can choose your email categories.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Permissions requested

Neatmail requests only the permissions it needs to label your emails and create drafts. Here's what each permission is used for:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Read email metadata | Used to classify incoming emails by subject, sender, and snippet |
| Apply labels / categories | Assigns the appropriate label to each email in your inbox |
| Create and manage labels | Creates Neatmail's category labels in your Gmail or Outlook account |
| Compose drafts | Creates reply drafts for emails that need a response |

No other inbox access is requested. Neatmail does not read, export, or store the full content of your emails.

<Warning>
  Neatmail only uses email content (subject, sender, and a short snippet) for classification. No raw email content is stored on Neatmail servers after processing.
</Warning>

## Disconnecting your account

To remove Neatmail's access to your email and delete all associated data, go to the **Delete Account** option in the sidebar footer and confirm the deletion. All your data — including labels, preferences, and integration settings — will be permanently removed.

You can also revoke Neatmail's OAuth access directly from your [Google account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) or [Microsoft account connected apps](https://account.microsoft.com/privacy/app-access) page at any time.
