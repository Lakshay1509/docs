---
title: "Quick Start"
description: "Get Neatmail connected and labeling your inbox in a few minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your email">
    After signing in, connect your Gmail or Outlook account. Neatmail uses OAuth — you'll be redirected to Google or Microsoft to authorize access. No passwords are stored.

    See [Connect Email](/connect-email) for a full walkthrough of the authorization flow and the permissions requested.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your email categories">
    An onboarding modal appears after your account is connected. Select at least one category from the 9 built-in options to tell Neatmail how to classify your inbox.

    The available categories are: Action Needed, Pending Response, Automated Alerts, Finance, Event Update, Discussion, Read Only, Resolved, and Marketing.

    You can update your selections at any time in **Settings > Labels**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable inbox monitoring">
    In **Settings > Labels**, toggle the **Monitor Inbox** switch to **Active**. This enables real-time watching — Neatmail begins processing new emails as they arrive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Neatmail starts labeling your emails">
    From this point, incoming emails are automatically classified and labeled directly in your Gmail or Outlook inbox. For emails that need a reply, a draft is created and waiting for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Select 5–6 categories for the best results. A broader selection gives Neatmail more context to accurately classify your incoming mail.
</Tip>
