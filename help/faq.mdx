---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Answers to frequently asked questions about Neatmail."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which email providers does Neatmail support?">
    Neatmail supports Gmail and Outlook (Microsoft 365).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Neatmail read or store my emails?">
    Neatmail processes email content only to classify it. No raw email content is stored — only metadata (labels applied, counts) is retained.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How quickly are emails labeled?">
    Emails are labeled in real time as they arrive — typically within seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create my own email categories?">
    Yes. In addition to the 9 built-in categories, you can create custom labels with your own name, color, and description from **Settings > Labels**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does AI draft generation work?">
    When a new email arrives that matches your draft sensitivity setting, Neatmail uses AI (GPT-4 Mini) to generate a reply draft in your tone, based on your draft prompt and signature. The draft appears in your Gmail or Outlook drafts folder.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a subscription to use Neatmail?">
    A 7-day free trial gives you full access. After the trial, a subscription is required for premium features including auto-labeling, AI drafts, and custom labels.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Neatmail on mobile?">
    Neatmail is a web app at [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app). Since labels are applied directly in Gmail and Outlook, you'll see your organized emails in any Gmail or Outlook app you use on mobile.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Go to **Billing** in the sidebar and click **Cancel subscription**. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
