---
title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Solutions to common issues with Neatmail, including labeling, AI drafts, and subscription problems."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Emails are not being labeled">
    1. Check that inbox monitoring is enabled. Go to **Settings > Labels** and confirm the watch toggle shows **Active**.
    2. Verify that at least one category is selected and saved.
    3. Confirm your email account is connected. Try disconnecting and reconnecting your account.
    4. Ensure you have an active subscription or free trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI drafts are not appearing">
    1. Check that drafts are enabled. Go to **Settings > Draft Preference** and confirm the **Enable Drafts** toggle shows **Active**.
    2. Review your draft sensitivity setting — **if actionable AND critical** is the most restrictive option and will generate fewer drafts.
    3. Verify your subscription is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Labels are not showing in Gmail or Outlook">
    Labels are created directly in your Gmail or Outlook account.

    - **Gmail** — check your Gmail labels list. Labels may take a few seconds to sync; try refreshing Gmail.
    - **Outlook** — categories appear in your Outlook categories list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I connected the wrong email account">
    Go to **Delete Account** in the sidebar footer to remove your account and start fresh. Alternatively, contact support through [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My subscription shows 'on_hold'">
    This usually means a payment issue. Go to **Billing** in the sidebar and open the **Customer Portal** to update your payment method.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  If your issue isn't listed here, reach out via the Neatmail website at [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app).
</Note>
