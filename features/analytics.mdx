The analytics dashboard gives you a clear picture of your email activity over time. All metrics are calculated from email metadata only — no email content is stored or analyzed.

## Accessing the dashboard

Navigate to **Home** in the sidebar to open the dashboard.

## Metrics and charts

### Summary stats

Three stat cards appear at the top of the dashboard, all scoped to the current month.

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Emails labeled this month | The total number of emails that Neatmail has categorized and labeled. |
| Time saved this month | Estimated time saved, calculated at 5 seconds per labeled email. Displayed in seconds, minutes, or hours depending on the total. |
| Average emails per day | Your labeled email count for the month divided by 30. |

### Label distribution

A chart showing the breakdown of emails by category. Use this to see which label types dominate your inbox.

### Email trends

A chart showing how your email volume has changed over time, helping you spot patterns or spikes in activity.

### Clutter score

A score that reflects how much noise is in your inbox — for example, a high proportion of Marketing or Read Only emails relative to actionable ones. A lower clutter score generally means a more focused inbox.

### Activity heatmap

A heatmap showing when emails arrive across days and hours. Use this to understand your busiest periods and plan focused work time around them.

<Note>
  Analytics are calculated from email metadata only. Neatmail does not store the content of your emails.
</Note>
