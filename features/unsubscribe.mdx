---
title: "Unsubscribe Manager"
description: "Identify and unsubscribe from unwanted email senders to reduce inbox clutter."
---

The Unsubscribe Manager helps you cut down on inbox noise by identifying senders you no longer want to hear from and unsubscribing from them in one place.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Unsubscribe">
    Navigate to **Unsubscribe** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review tracked senders">
    Browse the list of senders that have been tracked in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unsubscribe">
    Select any sender you want to stop receiving emails from and confirm the unsubscribe action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keeping your inbox clean

<Tip>
  Use the Unsubscribe Manager alongside auto labeling. Emails classified under the **Marketing** category are a good starting point — review those senders first and unsubscribe from any that are no longer relevant to you.
</Tip>
