AI Drafts monitors your incoming emails and automatically writes reply drafts for messages that need a response. Each draft is placed in your Gmail or Outlook drafts folder — you review it, adjust if needed, and send. Nothing is sent automatically.

<Note>
  AI Drafts requires an active Pro subscription.
</Note>

## How it works

When a new email arrives, Neatmail evaluates whether a reply is appropriate based on your sensitivity setting. If a draft should be generated, it uses your draft prompt and communication style to compose a reply in your tone. The draft appears in your email client's drafts folder within seconds.

## Draft sensitivity

The sensitivity setting controls when Neatmail generates a draft. Choose the option that matches how selective you want the feature to be.

| Option | When a draft is generated |
|---|---|
| Always draft | For every incoming email |
| If known sender AND directly addressed | Only when the email is from a recognized sender and addressed specifically to you |
| If actionable | When the email contains a task, request, or question that warrants a reply |
| If actionable AND critical | Only for high-priority actionable emails |

## Customization options

You can tailor how drafts are written and formatted from **Settings > Draft Preference**.

- **Draft prompt** — describe your communication style, goals, decision-making approach, or business context. The AI uses this to shape the tone and content of every draft. Supports up to 1000 characters.
- **Email signature** — your signature is appended to each draft. Copy it from your Gmail settings (not from a previously sent email) to ensure it renders correctly.
- **Font size** — set the default font size for draft text.
- **Font color** — set the default font color using a hex value or the color picker.

<Tip>
  Write a detailed draft prompt that reflects how you actually communicate. Include your role, how formal or casual you tend to be, and any phrases or approaches you prefer or want to avoid. The more context you give, the more accurate the drafts will be.
</Tip>

## Enabling AI Drafts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar and select **Draft Preference**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the toggle">
    Switch the toggle to **Active**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your preferences">
    Set your draft prompt, sensitivity level, signature, and formatting options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Update preferences** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
