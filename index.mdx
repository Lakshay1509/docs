Neatmail watches your inbox 24/7 and organizes it in real time. Incoming emails are automatically categorized with labels applied directly in Gmail or Outlook. When a reply is needed, Neatmail drafts it in your tone — ready to review and send.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [neatmail.app](https://www.neatmail.app) using your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your inbox">
    Link your Gmail or Outlook account with a single OAuth click. Neatmail requests only the permissions it needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your categories">
    Choose from 9 built-in email categories — or create your own custom labels to match your workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let Neatmail work">
    New emails are labeled the moment they arrive. Drafts appear in your inbox for replies that need a response.
  </Step>
</Steps>
