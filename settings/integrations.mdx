Neatmail integrates with external services to deliver email notifications and extend your workflow beyond the inbox. Currently, Telegram is the supported integration.

## Telegram

Connect your Telegram account to receive instant notifications about labeled emails directly in your Telegram chats.

### How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Navigate to **Integrations** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Telegram card">
    Locate the Telegram card on the Integrations page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the integration">
    Toggle the switch on the Telegram card. You will be redirected to the NeatMail Telegram bot to complete the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure notification rules">
    Once connected, configure which labeled emails trigger a Telegram notification using the rules panel that appears on the card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disconnecting

Toggle the switch off on the Telegram card at any time to disconnect your Telegram account. Your notification rules will be removed.

<Note>
  More integrations may be added in the future. Check the Integrations page for the latest available connections.
</Note>
